Volunteer with us





In TryMeTree we really value the commitment and help of our volunteers. Volunteers started this organisation and have remained the foundation blocks throughout our growth. Our whole organisation started with three volunteers; Nicole, Melanie and Miranda. They were all passionate environmental activists who all happened to enrol in a coding course with Vodafone. They were forced to work together, and they realised their common goal in making a difference and started this business.
Even though our numbers are always growing at a rapid rate, we are always looking for more people to get involved, so that our union makes us an unstoppable force and actually causes change in our world. Every year we have new volunteers joining our team. Last year, we had over 8000 new 'treers' and so far in 2019, over 12000 new volunteers have joined our team in the past 9 months.
If you are interested in getting involved, fill in the form below and we'll get in contact as soon as we can. We are really flexible with locations and times that our volunteers work and are always open to new ideas and projects. We encourage you to spread awareness about our organisation and our goals, so that we can keep building this network of people, friends, wanting to make a difference in not only the world for ourselves, but also for future generations.
